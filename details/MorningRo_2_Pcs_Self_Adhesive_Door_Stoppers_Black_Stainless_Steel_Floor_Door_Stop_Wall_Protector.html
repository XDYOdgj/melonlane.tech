MorningRo Self Adhesive Door Stoppers

Keep your doors securely in place without drilling or screwing. These sleek, modern door stoppers are the perfect blend of style and durability, offering reliable protection for your floors and walls.

Why Choose MorningRo?

Designed with a semicircular shape, these door stops gently guide the door away from walls, preventing damage and ensuring smooth operation. The brushed stainless steel material is not only sturdy but also resistant to rust, giving you a long-lasting solution that maintains its elegant look over time.

The included rubber cushion helps absorb impact, reducing noise and preserving your door and surrounding surfaces. Whether for your living room, bedroom, bathroom, or office, their compact design makes them ideal for tight spaces and easy to install in any setting.

Key Features

  • Self adhesive installation - no drilling required
  • Semicircular design prevents door from hitting walls
  • Sturdy and durable brushed stainless steel material
  • Smooth, shiny finish adds a touch of sophistication
  • Silent operation with thickened plastic rubber cushion
  • Perfect for multiple rooms and surfaces

Enhance Your Home or Office with Style and Safety

These door stoppers are the ideal choice for both new and existing installations. They offer a modern aesthetic with a black finish that complements any interior design while providing an easy, clean, and effective way to keep your doors in place.
